I am a naturally curious person who loves diving into global affairs, political systems, and philosophical frameworks. Keeping up with world history, macro-geopolitics, and random general knowledge trivia helps me keep my mind sharp and look at complex problems from multiple angles.

### 🏎️ Formula 1
I am a massive F1 fan. The intersection of cutting-edge automotive engineering, aerodynamics, and split-second strategic decision-making makes every race weekend fascinating to watch.

### ✏️ Sketching
Science demands absolute precision, so I turn to sketching as a creative outlet. It provides a completely different way to observe and interpret the world. 

### 🎸 Ukulele

### 🎬 Watchlist
